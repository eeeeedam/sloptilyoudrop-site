A letter from Ellie

I first noticed it in small, innocent ways. My social media feeds grew hollow. An aunt DM'd me AI puppies encouraging me to "never give up." My mom sent me a toucan made of sashimi. Cool. A trickle then became a floodgate of passable slop permeating every corner of my online experience. What I saw was mimicking humanity, but without its randomness, patina, imperfections.

As much as I try to avoid it, I CAN'T STOP THINKING ABOUT SLOP. I'm less consumed by haunting images of Shrimp Jesus or "Fruit Love Island" than what slop is doing to US — the human US. What are we accepting as passable entertainment, art, literature, music, companionship? And what will we grow to accept as this is further normalized and indistinguishable from human work?

That's what I'm tracking. Only lying people/agents will claim to have a crystal ball, but keeping score isn't hard. A few weeks of Claudecode (!) later, The Slop Galaxy was born. A way to visualize & track key moments like when AI-generated slop entered a courtroom, a newsroom, a presidential timeline, or a child's video stream.

Together, these start to paint a picture of how this tech is changing the terms of what we trust, consume, and tolerate.

Maybe you, too, have been watching closely. Maybe you're blissfully touching grass or carelessly surfing the web. Either way, something's shifting, and you're invited to join my attempt to understand what and how — in real time.

slop — digital content of low quality, produced in quantity by means of artificial intelligence. Merriam-Webster Word of the Year, 2025.

How to read it

Every node is a documented moment. Here's what you're looking at.

01
Size indicates impact

The larger the node, the higher its impact score — a curated judgment of how much this moment changed the world. Scores run 1 to 10.

02
Slop vs. Spill is indicated by placement

The sloppier the moment, the lower the node is on the y-axis. The more of a spill — a consequence, a reckoning, legislation — the higher it is.

03
Every node has a story behind it

Tap any bubble in the galaxy to expand the full story — what happened, what it means, the source, and sometimes an editor's note from Ellie. An arc connects the key moments into a through-line.
